About Scam Shield

An intelligent cybersecurity platform designed to help users detect scams, phishing attacks, fake recruiters, malicious URLs, and online fraud before they cause harm.

Millions of users receive fake internship offers, fraudulent SMS messages, phishing links and scam emails every day. Scam Shield helps identify threats instantly.
